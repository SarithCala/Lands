HOW WE CAN PROTECT THE ENVIROMENT?

Protect nature, it's unique

It is important to become aware of this problem, the home of all human beings, our home, is at risk.

1Unfortunately...

Unfortunately, humans have not been very good stewards of the Earth over the years. To protect the environment and preserve the planet for our children and future generations, we all need to take proactive steps toward cleaner living habits. For this reason, we have compiled very useful information to start improving as human beings in the protection of our environment

How to help?

So, what can we do?

1. Conserve energy

2. Reduce, reuse and recycle

3. Conserve water

4. Plant a tree

5. Walk, bike or carpool

6. Use eco-friendly products

7. Start a compost pile

8. Plant vegetables

9. Shop wisely

10. Don't send chemicals into our waterways.

11. Please don't throw the trash on the streets

12. Educate others

13. Be volunteer
